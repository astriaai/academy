Three things to know.

  • What works as a reference — any clean shot of the product. Polished studio capture is ideal; even a phone snap on the cutting table will fly. The cleaner the source, the more faithful the rotation.
  • Format & finish — portrait 3:4 at studio quality, looped to drop straight into the PDP, the lookbook, or the Reels grid. Matches a fashion grid out of the box.
  • Per-product cost — pennies per rotation. Cheap enough to A/B test two backdrops or a half-step in the angle before locking the listing shot.
